Recording Audio & Video Clips: Click on Video/Audio button to record video/audio clips. Enter the recipient’s e-mail address in the screen that follows before recording your clip.

Share As: These are privacy options for your clips. Private clips would only be accessible to the receiver. Public clips can be viewed by anyone using iXprez. Selecting the ‘Both’ option would make the clip’s Privacy Public after it is received.

Filters: Add various filters to your video by selecting the filters at the bottom of the video record screen.

Featured Videos: Videos featuring in the carousel of iXprez dashboard and have been picked by iXprez staff.

Trending Videos: Videos that have been most viewed by users recently

Recent Videos: Videos that have been uploaded recently

Private Videos: Videos that have been sent to you privately

Notifications: Updates of recently uploaded videos can be received by enabling notifications
